Phase 2 · Battery Storage
Add a Battery System — Capture Every Kilowatt at the Best Rate

Once your station is operational, we help you apply for California's Self-Generation Incentive Program (SGIP) to install a battery storage system. For qualifying tribal sites in high fire-threat districts or disadvantaged communities, SGIP can cover up to 100% of the battery cost. The battery charges overnight at low off-peak rates, then powers your chargers during expensive peak hours — eliminating demand charges and capturing pricing arbitrage.
